# Go-to-Market Strategy — Scaling Sales in a Startup

## Executive Summary
This document outlines the GTM strategy designed for a venture-backed startup to build a repeatable sales motion from scratch.

## Sales Framework: The 5-Step Engine
1. **Discovery:** Understand customer needs and pain points
2. **Demo:** Showcase product value proposition
3. **Proposal:** Present tailored solution and pricing
4. **Negotiation:** Address objections and finalize terms
5. **Close:** Secure commitment and handoff to customer success

## Key Metrics Tracked
- Pipeline velocity
- Conversion rates by stage
- Average deal size
- Sales cycle length
- Rep quota attainment
